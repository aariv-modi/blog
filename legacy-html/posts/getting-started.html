Getting Started with Writing

Published on May 5, 2026

The hardest part of writing is starting. Once a draft exists, even a bad one, the rest is just editing.

A few things that help

  1. Lower the stakes. Write the kind of post you'd send to a friend, not the kind you'd submit for publication.
  2. Pick small topics. One idea per post. If you have two, that's two posts.
  3. Publish before it's ready. Done is better than perfect, and feedback beats guessing.

The best writing habit is whichever one you'll actually keep. Short and frequent beats long and rare.
