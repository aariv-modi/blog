Welcome to the first post on this blog.

This is a small corner of the internet where I plan to share notes, ideas, and the occasional longer essay. Topics will probably range across whatever I happen to be thinking about — usually something between software, books, and the everyday.

The site is built with React, TypeScript, and Vite, and deployed on Vercel. It's intentionally simple: a list of posts, and not much else. The goal is to keep the friction of writing as low as possible.

Thanks for stopping by. More soon.
